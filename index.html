I miss you, Malaika.

You entered me without permission and stayed without promise. No touch, no questions—just a quiet taking of space I didn’t know could be taken. I never paused to wonder who you are to the world or whether you would remain in mine. The moment you existed, everything else went silent.

Your words don’t reach me—they undo me. Time collapses when I think of you; seconds lose their meaning. I live in the echo of you, in the spaces between breaths, carrying you like something sacred and unbearable.

Every thought returns to you. Always you.

Infinite Echo
